Melodics is the **dominant MIDI learning platform** with 250,000+ users, strong partnerships (Roland, Serato), and a comprehensive lesson library (1200+ lessons, 180+ songs). They excel at **gamified practice** and **real-time MIDI feedback** across keyboards, pads, and drums.

**SP404 Academy Opportunity:** Build the **only dedicated SP-404MK2 learning platform** that combines hardware-specific tutorials, sampling workflows, beat-making techniques, and AI-powered sample discovery ‚Äî areas Melodics completely ignores.
